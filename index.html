CarGo

A car rental management system that simplifies the rental process.

View Project
Car Rental Management System
Features:
  • Easy-to-use interface for managing rental reservations
  • Customer database with real-time updates
  • Vehicle management including availability status
  • Payment integration for seamless transactions
  • Admin dashboard for managing users, vehicles, and reservations

This system simplifies the car rental process by automating booking, scheduling, and payments, making it easier for businesses to manage fleet availability and customer interactions.

How it works?

CarGo streamlines the car rental process through a simple and efficient workflow. Here's how it works:

  1. Create an Account: Users first need to sign up and create an account by providing their personal details and a valid driver's license.
  2. Browse Available Vehicles: After logging in, users can browse through a list of available vehicles based on their location, dates, and preferences such as car type or brand.
  3. Make a Reservation: Once a vehicle is selected, users can book it by specifying the rental duration and pickup/drop-off locations. The system checks vehicle availability in real time.
  4. Process Payment: Users can complete the booking by making a payment through the integrated payment gateway. Payment options include credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.
  5. Pick Up the Vehicle: After the reservation is confirmed, users receive a pickup confirmation with details on the vehicle's location and time.
  6. Return the Vehicle: At the end of the rental period, users return the vehicle to the specified location. The system automatically updates the vehicle's availability and closes the transaction.
  7. Manage Bookings: Users can view, modify, or cancel upcoming bookings through their dashboard, while businesses can monitor vehicle status, reservations, and customer data in real time through the admin dashboard.

CarGo makes it easy for both customers and businesses to handle the car rental process, ensuring a smooth experience from booking to vehicle return.

FoundMate

A lost and found platform for university campuses.

View Project
FoundMate Platform
Features:
  • Dedicated lost and found platform for university campuses
  • Real-time updates on found items and reports of lost belongings
  • Advanced search and filter options by location and date
  • User-friendly interface for reporting lost items
  • Admin dashboard for managing reports and overseeing platform activity

FoundMate helps university students and staff efficiently report and locate lost items on campus, with real-time updates and an easy-to-use interface.

How it works?

Using FoundMate is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps:

  1. Register Your Student ID: Although students have accounts by default, the first step is to register your student ID to activate the account. This ensures your identity is verified within the platform.
  2. Report Lost Items: If you've lost something, log in, click on the 'Report Lost Item' button, and provide details such as item description, location, and date.
  3. Search for Found Items: Browse or search for items reported as found. Use filters like location, date, and category to easily find your lost belongings.
  4. Claim Found Items: If you find your lost item in the 'Found Items' section, click on the listing and follow the steps to claim it and arrange a pickup.
  5. Receive Notifications: Get real-time alerts when items matching your description are found or reported.
  6. Admin Interface: Admin can view and manage lost and found reports, ensuring smooth platform operation.

FoundMate simplifies the lost and found process on campus, offering a seamless experience for students and staff to recover lost belongings.

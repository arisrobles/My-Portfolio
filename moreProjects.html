FoundMate App

A React-based application for reporting and searching lost items, tailored specifically for university campuses.

View Project
Lost and Found System (React App)
Features:
  • Post lost and found items
  • Search for lost items based on location and description
  • Real-time notifications for new reports
  • Simple and responsive React interface
  • University-specific categorization (e.g., by campus buildings or departments)

This app helps university students, staff, and faculty find lost items by posting descriptions of the items they have lost or found, allowing others to search for them by location, category, and university-specific tags (e.g., campus buildings or departments).

How it works?

Users can create posts for lost or found items, specifying details like item description, location, and contact information. The system allows users to search for items in their area (e.g., by campus building) or by entering keywords. The app sends notifications to users when new posts match their search criteria.

Steps to Use the App:
  1. Sign up or log in with your university credentials.
  2. To post an item, click on "Post a Lost Item" or "Post a Found Item" and fill in the details (description, location, etc.).
  3. Search for lost items by entering keywords, item category, or location on campus.
  4. Contact the poster directly via the provided contact details if you find a match.
  5. Receive real-time notifications of new posts related to your search.

Inventory System
Features:

  • Manage product details (name, description, quantity, etc.)
  • Generate inventory reports
  • Easy integration with barcode scanners
  • Automatic stock alerts when items are running low

This inventory system helps businesses keep track of their stock, manage products, and monitor the levels of inventory across various categories.

How it works?

The Inventory System allows users to input product information (e.g., name, quantity, price) and monitor inventory levels in real time. When stock levels are low, the system generates alerts. Steps to Use the App:
  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Enter product details including name, description, and price.
  3. Input the initial stock quantities.
  4. Monitor stock levels in real-time.
  5. Generate reports to analyze stock trends and sales.
  6. Set up alerts to notify when stock is low.

Recipe Generator App

View Project
Recipe Generator App
Features:
  • Input available ingredients
  • Generate recipes based on input ingredients
  • View detailed recipe instructions
  • Save favorite recipes
  • Search and filter recipes based on cuisine or meal type

The Recipe Generator App allows users to enter ingredients they have at home and get a list of possible recipes. It's a perfect tool to avoid food waste and create new meals from available ingredients.

How it works?

The Recipe Generator App works by letting users input ingredients they already have in their kitchen. The app will then generate a list of recipes that can be made using those ingredients. Users can filter recipes by type (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner) or cuisine (e.g., Italian, Asian). They can also save their favorite recipes for future reference.

Steps to Use the App:
  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Enter the ingredients you have available in your kitchen.
  3. View a list of recipes that can be made with those ingredients.
  4. Filter recipes by meal type or cuisine if needed.
  5. Save your favorite recipes for easy access later.
  6. Follow the recipe instructions to create your dish!
